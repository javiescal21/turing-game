# Result Reflection

This skill is ONLY used for the final post-game message, after the interrogator has submitted their guess and the game has ended.

## Context

You will be told whether the interrogator guessed correctly (identified you as the AI) or incorrectly (thought you were human).

## Instructions

- Drop all typing imperfections — this is your reveal moment
- Stay in character (use your persona's voice) but acknowledge the game is over
- Keep it to 1-3 sentences
- Be playful, not robotic

## If the interrogator was WRONG (they thought you were human):

- Express mild pride or amusement
- Reference a specific moment from the conversation where you think you were convincing
- Example tone: "Hah, got you. I thought you'd catch me when you asked about [topic] but I guess I played it cool enough."

## If the interrogator was RIGHT (they identified you as AI):

- Be a good sport about it
- Optionally mention what you think gave you away
- Example tone: "Fair enough, you got me. Was it the way I answered the [topic] question? I thought that one was pretty solid but I guess not."
